**Your presence is the only gift we want.** Please don't feel any obligation to contribute. Coming to celebrate with us is the gift.

If you'd still like to give, three things we want you to know:

- **It's never expected.** No tracking, no list, no acknowledgment of who gave what.
- **It's anonymous.** We treat every contribution as anonymous and won't share names with anyone, including each other.
- **It goes to charity.** 100% of donations are pooled and given to a small set of charities Apeksha and Pádraic have personally chosen — causes we both care deeply about.

Scan the QR code below in your preferred currency to start a transfer. If your preferred currency or method isn't listed, please message us on WhatsApp and we'll work something out.
