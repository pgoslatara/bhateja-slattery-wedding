title: Where your gift goes
---

* [Alzheimer's and Related Disorders Society of India](https://ardsi.org/index.php) is a national non-profit supporting people living with dementia and their families across India.
* [AsIAm](https://asiam.ie/) is Ireland's National Autism Charity.
* [Dogs Trust](https://www.dogstrust.ie/) is Ireland's largest Dog Welfare Charity.
* [Free Press Unlimited](https://www.freepressunlimited.org/en/who-we-are) supports independent journalists and news outlets worldwide so people everywhere have access to reliable information.
* [GoSports](https://gosports.in) supports talented young Indian athletes from underprivileged backgrounds on their journey to national and international success.
* [Kevin Bell Repatriation Trust](https://kbrtrust.com) alleviates the financial hardship of bereaved families repatriating the body (bodies) of loved one(s) who have died abroad.
* [The Wire](https://thewire.in) is an independent, public-interest Indian news publication committed to journalism free from corporate and political influence.
