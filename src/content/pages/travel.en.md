The wedding takes place in Gurgaon, just outside Delhi. **Indira Gandhi International Airport (DEL)** is the closest major airport, around 15–25 km from most Gurgaon hotels.

## Direct flights to Delhi

These airports from selected parts of Europe have **non-stop** flights to Delhi. Everywhere else (including Brussels, Luxembourg, Dublin, and the rest of Ireland and northern France) you will need at least one connection.

| From | Airport | Airlines |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Netherlands | Amsterdam Schiphol (AMS) | KLM, Air India |
| Germany | Frankfurt (FRA) | Lufthansa, Air India |
| Germany | Munich (MUC) | Lufthansa |
| France | Paris Charles de Gaulle (CDG) | Air France, Air India |

If you are flying from Belgium, Luxembourg, or Ireland, the easiest connections are usually via Amsterdam, Frankfurt, Paris, or one of the Gulf hubs (Doha, Dubai, Abu Dhabi).

## Getting from the airport to your hotel

Delhi airport is busy and arrivals can feel overwhelming after a long flight. The **safest and most reliable** option for first-time visitors is the **official Delhi Traffic Police prepaid taxi**:

- Look for the **police-branded prepaid taxi counter** inside the Terminal 3 arrivals hall (after customs, before you exit into the crowds). Book here rather than outside.
- Tell the officer your destination (hotel name and Gurgaon sector). You pay a **fixed fare** upfront — roughly **₹600–₹900** (~**€5–8**) to Gurgaon, including the night surcharge and inter-state toll — by cash (INR) or card.
- You will receive a two-part receipt with a taxi registration number. Walk to the prepaid taxi lane outside and find the matching car.
- **Important:** only hand the receipt to the driver **after** you arrive and your bags are out of the boot. The driver needs it to get paid, so it is your guarantee they take you to the right place.

If you have mobile data set up (eSIM or local SIM), **Uber** and **Ola** also work well. The pickup zone is in the **Multi-Level Car Park** opposite Terminal 3 arrivals; follow the "App-Based Cabs" signs (a 3–5 minute walk). Expect ₹500–₹1,500 (~€4–14) to Gurgaon depending on time of day.

**Ignore the touts** who approach you outside the terminal offering taxis or "help with your Uber". Only speak to staff standing behind the official counters.
