question: What Hindi words and phrases should I know?
---

Here are a few useful Hindi words to help you get into the spirit of things. The capitalised syllable carries the stress, and *aa* is a long *a* like in "father".

- **paani** (PAA-nee): water.
- **achcha** (UCH-cha): hard to pin down; works for okay, oh, I see, alright, good, and most other shades of agreement or acknowledgement.
- **shaadi** (SHAA-dee): wedding.
- **shaadi mubarak** (SHAA-dee moo-BAA-rak): congratulations on the wedding.
- **namastey** (nuh-MUS-tay): hello and goodbye in one, said with hands pressed together.
- **nahi** (nuh-HEE): no.
- **haan** (HAAN): yes, with a soft nasal at the end.
